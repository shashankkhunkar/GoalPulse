## Overview
GoalPulse is a FIFA World Cup 2026 score, schedule, standings and news app. We
designed it to be privacy-friendly: **GoalPulse does not require an account and
does not collect, store, or share personal information.**

## Information we do NOT collect
We do not collect your name, email, phone number, contacts, location, photos, or
any personally identifying information. There is no login or user profile.

## Information stored on your device only
Your preferences — selected language, country, time zone, theme, and notification
settings — are stored **locally on your device** using Android preferences. This
data never leaves your device and is removed when you uninstall the app.

## Third-party data sources
To show live scores and news, GoalPulse retrieves publicly available World Cup
data from third-party services, including ESPN, football-data.org, API-Football,
and BBC Sport. When the app requests this data, your device's IP address is
necessarily visible to those services as part of a standard internet request, and
their use of it is governed by their own privacy policies. GoalPulse does not send
them any personal information about you, and does not store these requests.

## Notifications
If you enable match notifications, they are generated based on World Cup match
data. We do not use these to collect information about you.

## Analytics & advertising
GoalPulse contains **no third-party analytics SDKs and no advertising**. We do not
track your usage.

## Children's privacy
GoalPulse is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any
data from children.

## Data security
Because GoalPulse does not collect or transmit personal data, there is no personal
data for us to secure on a server. Preference data remains on your device.

## Changes to this policy
We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected
by updating the effective date above.
